Header is it's own component to keep the logo and a compact version of the Certificate

SideMenu mostly keeps the buttons that links to various routes in body
SideMenu directly changes the content presented

Body has multiple states. Posts and Profile for now.

Posts will have the CreatePost component at the top of it. Below it will list all posts in a Post component

Post is composed of a Certificate of who posted, and what they posted, and lists maxium three Comment components
You can also Comment on this Post.

Comment is composed of a Certificate of who commented, and what they wrote.

Certificate is mostly just a profile picture, with a username and status.
It has two states, a compact state and a expanded state

Profile is info about the current profile used. They may change any related info to that profile.
This is hooked to the /contact endpoint, and will generate PUT requests
